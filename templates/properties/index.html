About Us

Introduction

Elephant Castle Properties is a dynamic and innovative real estate investment consultancy. We are focused on delivering excellence in value management, project management, estate consulting and property management services. Our range of services spans the scope of the real estate investor's needs, from simple conveyance to full management of developed property and disposal as the case may be. We have concrete experience in managing residential and commercial properties both in and outside of Lagos. Our services ensure that our client's realties are kept in functional and profitable order, ensuring or exceeding the returns on our client's investment in their property portfolio. Well-managed property assets cost less, deliver better returns and project the perfect image for clients. With an impressive track record and extensive experience, Elephant Castle's management team has enabled developers, owners and occupiers alike to ensure both efficient day-to-day operations and the best possible economic returns.

GOAL

We place all resources at our disposal in to the management of our realties. Our experience in asset management, be it residential or commercial, affirms that we achieve or exceed the desired result.

HOW IT WORKS

Property Management

Our experienced and committed team manages commercial, Residential and Industrial Properties of all sorts. Management encompasses all aspects of routine day-to-day, month to month and year-to-year services including tenant selection, the collection and accounting of rents, insurance and service charges, the enforcement of Landlord and Tenants repair obligations and other lease concords, the supervision of service and maintenance contracts and the implementation and record of rent and lease renewals. Our Surveyor's advice on planned schedules of maintenance and building improvement, ensuring Landlords are kept appraised on likely future expenditure.

Estate Consulting

Our firm is involved in providing unbiased end-to-end advisory services on the real estate market to our clients. This we carry out by researching real estate markets to understand trends and market fundamentals in order to anticipate changes in the market. This market intelligence is critical to our keeping ahead of the market, remaining competitive and elevating the quality of our real estate investment decision-making on behalf of our clients. Our aim here is geared towards providing efficient, reliable and professional real estate advisory services to meet the diverse needs of our clients and aid them in potential decision-making.
